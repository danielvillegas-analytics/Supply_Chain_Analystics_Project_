# Supply Chain — End-to-End Analytics Project

Overview

End-to-end supply chain analytics project covering global markets from February 2015 to January 2018, analyzing operations, sales and customer behavior across three tools.
________________________________________
Tools Used

Excel + Power Query — Data cleaning, pivot tables, charts, slicers and forecasting · View Workbook
MySQL — 25+ queries covering aggregations, CTEs and window functions · View Script
Power BI — Data modeling, DAX measures and interactive dashboard · View Dashboard
________________________________________
What Was Analyzed

Operations & Shipping Performance On-time vs. late delivery rate · Shipping delay by mode · Order status breakdown · Revenue split by delivery outcome
Sales & Profitability Revenue and profit by market, category and department · Top products · Monthly trends · Running total · Market contribution % · CTEs and window functions for rankings
Customer Behavior Profit and discount by segment · New vs. repeat customers · Orders by country · Top spenders ranked with RANK() and NTILE()
________________________________________
Key Findings

Western Europe led total sales at $10.9M, nearly double the next region
54%+ of orders carry a late delivery risk, mostly in Standard and Second Class shipping
Profit margin held steady at ~10–11% throughout the entire period
Consumer segment drives the most orders; Corporate yields higher profit per order
Top 10% of customers account for a disproportionate share of total revenue
